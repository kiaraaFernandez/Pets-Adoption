Welcome

We are a shelter in Punilla for animals in need. We provide veterinary care and are looking for responsible families. We hope you can connect with one of them.

Adoption Process

Our adoption process aims to make sure every pet finds a responsible home that suits their needs.

1

Explore

Meet our available pets and find out which one could be your ideal companion.

2

Form

Fill out the adoption application indicating the pet you're interested in.

3

Review

Our team will look over your request and get in touch with you.

4

Interview

We did a short interview to get to know the environment where the pet will live.

5

Adoption

Once the application is approved, we coordinate the delivery and adjustment to the new home.

6

Follow-up

We continue supporting the family to ensure a successful adaptation.

Basic Pet Care

💧 Fresh water

Make sure your pet always has access to clean, fresh water to stay hydrated.

🍖 Feeding

It provides a balanced diet appropriate for their age, size, and activity level.

🏥 Health

Keep up to date on vaccinations, veterinary check-ups, and deworming to prevent diseases.

🎾 Physical exercise

Play and physical activity help maintain good physical and emotional health.

🧼 Hygiene

Keep their space clean and provide the necessary care for their fur, teeth, and nails.

❤️ Love

Pets need attention, companionship, and affection to develop happily and safely.
